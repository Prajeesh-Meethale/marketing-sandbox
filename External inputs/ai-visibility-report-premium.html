Module 04 — The Machine Layer

What AI sees on fernbridge.io directly

Free to check, and fully within Fernbridge's own control — no dependency on outside coverage.

No direct line to the models

MechanismNo llms.txt, no robots.txt blocking — crawlers can enter, but nothing points them to what matters.
CostModels default to third-party pages about Fernbridge instead of what the company says about itself.
Publish llms.txtQuick win · low effort

Present, but not quotable

MechanismModels quote pages that already read like an answer. Fernbridge's pages sell — they don't answer.
CostThis is why Deputy gets quoted directly and Fernbridge gets left off the same list entirely.
Rewrite pricing and comparison pages, add FAQ schemaMedium effort

Possibly invisible to non-JS crawlers

MechanismNo SSR or noscript fallback on feature pages — a bot that skips JavaScript may see nothing there.
CostContent already written may functionally not exist to a share of the crawlers reaching the site.
Add SSR or noscript fallbackHigh effort · needs a developer
All clear HTTPS, page weight, and language declaration all pass. This was never a broken-site problem.
