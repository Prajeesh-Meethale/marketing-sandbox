### 3. Step 4: Final Investigation Report — Consulting Storytelling vs. SEO Technical Lingo
* **Mr. X Proposal**: Translate technical jargon into executive consulting language.
  - *Instead of:* `"llms.txt missing"` → *Say:* `"AI receives very little structured guidance about your company."`
  - *Instead of:* `"FAQ Schema Missing"` → *Say:* `"Your expertise is difficult for AI systems to confidently understand."`
* **AI Evaluation & Validation**:
  - **CRITICAL VALIDATION**: This is the single most important product distinction. Prospect executives don't buy "schema markup"; they buy **market positioning and deal capture**.
  - **How the Engine Handles This**: Our existing Zod `Insight` domain model explicitly separates technical findings from business narratives using 3 mandatory fields:
    1. `narrative`: What the data shows in plain business English.
    2. `whyThisMatters`: The strategic reason an executive should care.
    3. `businessImpact`: The top-line revenue/lead impact.
  - **Translation Mapping for Technical Audit Checks**:
    - `Robots.txt Blocked` → *"Major AI search engines are actively blocked from discovering your products."*
    - `Schema Markup Missing` → *"AI models struggle to accurately attribute your core services and authority."*
    - `llms.txt Missing` → *"AI systems receive zero direct guidance on how to represent your brand."*
    - `SSR / JS Render Block` → *"AI web crawlers cannot read your website content during live searches."*
